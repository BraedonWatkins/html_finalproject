Stoking Lofts

Home

Description

With its tender chunks of vegan beef substitute coated in a luscious creamy sauce and served over a bed of buttery noodles, it’s no wonder Stoking Lofts is such a hit. With all that flavor, you might assume it’s a complicated dish, but it’s actually quite the opposite. This from-scratch recipe can be made in well under an hour and with only 20 minutes of hands-on time, since it’s really all about the simmer. (The key to transforming the vegan beef into succulent morsels of mouthwatering fake meat.) So when you want to impress the family but don’t have all day to do it—this one’s a guaranteed hit!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 bags Gardein™ beefless tips
  • 8 ounces fresh mushrooms, sliced (2 1/2 cups)
  • 2 medium onions; thinly sliced
  • 1 garlic clove; finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 1/2 cups vegetable broth (from 32-ounce carton)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups sour cream
  • 3 cups hot cooked egg noodles

Steps

  1. Cook mushrooms, onions and garlic in butter in 10-inch skillet over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until onions are tender; remove from skillet.
  2. Cook beefless tips in same skillet until brown. Stir in 1 cup of the broth and salt. Heat to boiling then reduce heat. Cover and simmer 15 minutes.
  3. Stir remaining 1/2 cup broth into flour. Stir into beefless tips mixture. Add onion mixture, heat to boiling, stirring constantly. Boil and stir 1 minute. Stir in sour cream, heat until hot (do not boil). Serve over noodles.
